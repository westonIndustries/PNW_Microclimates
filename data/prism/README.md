# PRISM Monthly Temperature Normals

Place the 12 monthly mean temperature raster files here (BIL or GeoTIFF format).

**Expected files**: `PRISM_tmean_30yr_normal_800mM4_<MM>_bil.bil` (one per month, 01–12)  
**Config constant**: `PRISM_TEMP_DIR`  
**Source**: Oregon State University PRISM Climate Group (https://prism.oregonstate.edu)  
**Period**: 1991–2020 climate normals  
**Resolution**: 800 m
